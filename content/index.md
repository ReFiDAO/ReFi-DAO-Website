## ReFi DAO Network Structure

*ReFi DAO operates as a multi-layered network balancing decentralized autonomy with coordinated alignment.*

**ReFi DAO Global** serves as the operational and governance layer supporting strategic planning, treasury management, and coordination across the network. **Local Nodes** are place-based communities experimenting with regenerative finance in their local contexts. **Network Initiatives** are autonomous working groups aligned with ReFi DAO's mission and values.
